Georgiy Shakhgildyan - Glass Science and Photonics Research

Georgiy Shakhgildyan is a PhD in Chemical Engineering and Associate Professor at Mendeleev University of Chemical Technology in Moscow, Russia. His research focuses on optical glass-ceramics, photonic glasses, plasmonic nanoparticles, and femtosecond laser writing in glass materials.

Research Highlights

Dr. Shakhgildyan's work includes designing and synthesizing new optical glasses, developing plasmonic nanoparticles with rare-earth ions in glass, and advancing femtosecond laser writing techniques for creating micro-optics in glass. He regularly delivers lectures and seminars on glass science and photonics, and serves as an editor for special issues in his field.

Awards and Recognition

In 2023, Dr. Shakhgildyan received the Moscow Government Prize for Young Scientists for his pioneering work in laser-induced structural modification of optical materials for photonics and optoelectronics applications.

Professional Roles

He serves as an expert for the Russian Science Foundation (RSF) and the "Priority-2030" Academic Leadership Program. Dr. Shakhgildyan is also a member of editorial boards for multiple journals specializing in glass science and photonics.

Publications and Impact

With over 50 peer-reviewed publications and more than 600 citations, Dr. Shakhgildyan has an h-index of 14 (Google Scholar) and 13 (Scopus). His work contributes significantly to the fields of optical materials, laser physics, and glass-ceramics research.
